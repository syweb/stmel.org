Community Service

St. Mel School perpetuates a strong sense of Catholic service to the local community and globally. We strive to educate the whole child in an atmosphere of faith and academic excellence and nurture a life long commitment to serving others. Our goal is to help each student become a faith-filled steward.

Classroom Service Projects

All grades TK through Eighth participate in community service projects. Many of these service projects directly correlate to the curriculum, student learning expectations or a particular interest of the class. In addition, students in grades 7thand 8th are required to complete 20 hours per school year of community service to fulfill Confirmation requirements.
