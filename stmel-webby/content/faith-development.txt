<h2>Faith Development</h2>
<div class="col col-large">
  <p>St. Mel School is committed to integrating our Catholic faith with excellence in academics. </p>
  <p>Catholic faith and values are integrated into the life and culture of the community and support the spiritual, emotional, and social development of each student. Students are guided into becoming </p>
  <p>Active Catholics with self-respect, respect for others, and a reverence for all of Godâ€™s creation.</p>
</div><!-- col col-large end -->
<div class="col">
  <ul>
    <li>There is a comprehensive religion curriculum in grades TK thru 8 </li>
    <li>Each day begins with a shared prayer </li>
    <li>The children attend Mass with their class once a week </li>
    <li>Each class participates in a special school liturgy attended by all students and many members of the parent community. </li>
    <li>Preparation for the reception of the sacraments is part of the curriculum in the second grade (Reconciliation and First Eucharist) and the eighth grade (Confirmation). </li>
    <li>During the seasons of Advent and Lent the students attend a Reconciliation Service. </li>
    <li>Students have the opportunity to participate in the celebration of Mass as lectors, choir members, and altar servers. </li>
    <li>The Parish Priests are a valuable resource for the religious program.</li>
  </ul>
</div><!-- col end -->
