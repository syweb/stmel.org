<h2>Admission Process</h2>

<div class="col col-x-large">
  <p>St. Mel School admits students of any race, color and national or ethnic origin. Students who are parishioners of St. Mel and Divine Savior parishes receive admission priority. In addition, priority will be given to siblings of St. Mel School students. <strong>Grades One through Eight</strong> â€“ Parents must complete a New Family Registration Form for any student applying to grades One through Eight. An entrance assessment is given to interested students upon completing the New Family form to verify appropriate grade placement and to determine whether St. Mel School can meet the needs of the transfer student. Copies of recent report cards and standardized tests will be requested for review.</p>
  
  <h3>Kindergarten</h3>
  <p>Incoming Kindergartners must be 5 years old by September 1st and developmentally ready for Kindergarten. Parents of students with a birthday before November 1 may also apply. Parents of any student applying for Kindergarten must complete a New Family Registration Form. An entrance assessment is administered to all applicants to help determine individual readiness. Assessments occur in the Spring.</p> 
  <p>For more information on our program click <a href = "kindergarten.html">here.</a></p>
  
  <h3>Transitional Kindergarten (TK)</h3>
  <p>TK is designed to be a bonus year before Kindergarten. TK students should be 4 years old by September 1st, 2013. Parents of any student applying for TK must compete a New Family Registration Form</p>
  
  <p>For more information on our program click <a href = "transitional-kindergarten.html">here.</a></p>

Please call the school office at 916-967-2814 or e-mail <a href="mailto:office@stmelschool.org">office@stmelschool.org</a> to set up an appointment. These tours run approximately one hour and are for prospective students and parents. You will have an opportunity to learn more about student life and how St. Mel School integrates faith with academic excellence and meet the school principal, Mrs. Janet Nagel. She will be able to answer all your questions regarding admission criteria, Student Learning Expectations, Common Core Standards, student visitation day, financial aid, before and after school care, student clubs, athletics, technology, fine arts, etc.</p>
