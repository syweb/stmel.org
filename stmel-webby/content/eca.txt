<h2>Enrichment</h2>
<div class="col col-large">
  <p>St. Mel School offers an extensive extra-curricular athletic program,
	primarily through the PAL (Parochial Athletic League). The boys in sixth,
	seventh and eighth grades can play flag football. Boys and girls in fifth
	through eighth grades are able to participate in cross-country, volleyball,
	track and field events, while students in sixth through eighth grades can
	participate in golf and bowling. We have basketball teams for boys and girls
	in first through eighth grades. Soccer is available to all of the children
	and is administered by the Fair Oaks Soccer Club.</p>
	<p>These are some of the activities and opportunities that enrich the
	curriculum and contribute to the moral, intellectual, spiritual, emotional,
	social and physical growth of the students:</p>
