<p>Currently, St. Mel School is accepting applications for the upcoming school year. <ul>

<li>Baptismal Certificate</li>

<p>Registration for both returning and new families takes place in early May. Any students accepted for admissions after the May registration date will be registered by appointment. Registration fees are due at the time of registration.</p>
