<div class="header"></div><h2>Sacraments</h2>
<div class="col col-large">
  <h3>SACRAMENTS: FIRST COMMUNION </h3>
  <p>The Second graders at St. Mel School are given in-depth instruction in preparation for First Holy Communion which includes special evening Masses for parents and students. Students receive the First Holy Eucharist during a special liturgy which occurs on a Saturday morning in the Spring.  </p>
  <h3>THE RITE OF RECONCILIATION </h3>
  <p>The preparation and reception of this sacrament is an integral part of the Second grade curriculum.  A special celebratory function is held directly afterwards for students.  </p>
</div><!-- col col-large end -->
<div class="col">
  <h3>RECONCILIATION </h3>
  <p>Students in grades Three through Eight attend Reconciliation during the seasons of Advent and Lent.  </p>
  <h3>CONFIRMATION </h3>
  <p>The Eighth graders at St. Mel School are given in-depth instruction in preparation to receive the gifts of the Holy Spirit in the Sacrament of Confirmation. Students receive Confirmation during a special liturgy which occurs on a weekday evening in the Spring. A visiting Bishop gives this particular Sacrament to the Eighth graders. </p>
  </div><!-- col end -->
